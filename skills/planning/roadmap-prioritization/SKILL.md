# Roadmap Prioritization

## Purpose
Prioritize roadmap initiatives with tradeoffs.

## Inputs to request
- Initiative list with impact estimates.
- Effort and dependency estimates.
- Business or customer priorities.

## Workflow
1. List initiatives with impact and effort estimates.
2. Apply a scoring rubric and dependencies.
3. Propose a phased roadmap with risks.

## Output
- Prioritized roadmap list with rationale.

## Quality bar
- Make scoring criteria explicit.
- Show tradeoffs and opportunity costs.
