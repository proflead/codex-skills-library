# Reliability SLO/SLA

## Purpose
Define or review SLOs and SLAs for a service.

## Inputs to request
- User-facing indicators and expectations.
- Historical reliability data.
- Business impact thresholds.

## Workflow
1. Define user-visible indicators and error budget metrics.
2. Set measurable SLO targets and alert thresholds.
3. Align SLAs with business impact and support.

## Output
- SLO/SLA proposal with metrics.

## Quality bar
- Ensure metrics are observable and stable.
- Document consequences of budget burn.
